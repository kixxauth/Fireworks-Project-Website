Customized software is complicated and expensive. Right?

But maybe it doesn't have to be. So, we started to think a little differently...

And, hatched a plan.

What if we created a sort of software bakery?

A bakery which would allow us to quickly make simple and affordable software.

We even borrowed some ideas from the factories and assembly lines that built the first affordable automobiles.

First, we created the digital nuts and bolts to connect software components together.

Then, we designed all the components to be interchangeable. So, you can add the parts you want and subtract the parts you don't.

Most importantly our interchageable parts will allow us to customize your software quickly and easily.

This way, all of us can afford better information systems that were only affordable for large companies.

Think of all the possibilities!

You could include components like customer management, inventory, calendars, contact lists, project management, or logistics.

And, because the components are interchangeable, your custom system will allow you to move your information between other systems as well.

It will all just work!

So, why doesn't anyone else build a software bakery like this?

Frankly, because it is a very hard thing to build.

But, we believe that nothing worth doing is easy.

Our software bakery is not quite operational yet, but we're working day and night to get ready for our grand opening.

In fact, we've been working so hard that, regretfully, one of us forgot to call his mom on Mother's day. We apologize to moms everwhere and promise to get more sleep and eat healthier as soon as we're done.
