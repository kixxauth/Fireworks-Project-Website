The Fireworks Project is a small software firm that builds incredible productivity tools for your small or medium sized organization.

Software that just works.

Personalized for the way you work.

At a price that works.

It's time to think differently about your information systems.

Dream big and we'll build it.

Don't leave without subscribing to our free organizational development newsletter.

Making it Work is our e-journal for people like you -- People who dream big to make your organization more productive and a better place to work.

The Fireworks Project publishes Making it Work because our software has everything to do with organizational development. Our ideal customer is one who cares about creating a better organization and Making it Work covers topics that are important to you.

* Our privacy policy is to never share, rent, or sell your information to anyone, EVER.

Customized software is complicated and expensive.

But maybe it doesn't have to be.

So, we started to think a little differently.

What if we could create a software bakery that would bake software?

Each loaf of software could be personalized to the individual needs and tastes of our patrons.

When we stretched our imaginations even further, we found more ideas in the assembly lines that created the first affordable automobiles.

So, we designed our own set of digital nuts and bolts to build interchangeable software components with.

As it turns out, having interchangeable parts has been the key to making affordable goods since the industrial revolution.

The best part is that interchangeable components will allow you to add only the components you need, without getting stuck with the ones you don't.

This new way of building software will enable you to afford customized information systems that were once only affordable to very large companies.

Think of all the possibilities!

  • Customer service management.
  • Contact and sales lists.
  • Project management.
  • Inventory control.
  • Scheduling.
  • Logistics.

And, because the system is designed to be interchangeable, it will allow you to move your information between other systems seamlessly.

It will all just work.

Our artisan software bakery is not quite operational yet, but we're working day and night to get it done for you.

Every day is one day closer to the grand opening.

It's not easy, but we believe that nothing worth doing is easy.

While we're working, you should kick back with a copy of our e-journal. Just scroll on over to our subscription form and you can get on board with one click.
